## Operation

The Friendtech Bot features two main functionalities: Alerts and Trade, designed to provide real-time notifications and facilitate the buying and selling of keys quickly and efficiently.

After adding the Bot with the name: alertfriendbot on your Telegram, you can start by adding your first Friendtech user using the command /add (username or wallet). This will allow you to create your first custom alert. Then, you'll have access to three types of alerts:

**Key** - Receive alerts whenever someone purchases the key from this user. <br>
**Trade** - Be notified whenever this user buys or sells a key. <br>
**Deposit** - Receive an alert every time someone makes a deposit into this account. <br>

To start trading, you need to access the **/trade** command and create a specific Ethereum wallet for the bot. Remember that you'll need to have an available balance to make purchases. Buying and selling options will be displayed on the screen of each user in your /myuser list. Additionally, the My Holding list is generated automatically, allowing for more effective control and better management of keys.
